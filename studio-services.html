Remote Session & Mix Services

Professional studio tracks and polished mixes. Integrated with your project, wherever you are in the world.

What I Offer

Whether you need a skilled multi-instrumentalist to lay down tracks or a professional set of ears to shape your final mix, I can plug into your project remotely and deliver results that sound like they came out of a serious studio...because they did.

🥁

Session Recording

Send me your project files, demos, charts, or reference tracks and I'll import everything into my studio and record professional-quality parts to your specifications. Every track goes through high-end preamps, carefully selected microphones, and a treated recording environment.

Available instruments: Drumset, classical percussion, acoustic guitar, electric guitar, bass, keyboards/synths, lap steel, mandolin, or any combination across multiple parts.

I work from whatever you have — a rough demo, a chord chart, a reference playlist, or just a creative direction. The goal is to serve the song and your vision.

🎚️

Mixing & Mastering

Send me your multi-track recordings and I'll deliver a polished, balanced mix that translates across all playback media. Whether you're starting from a rough mix that needs a professional pass or sending raw stems that need everything built from scratch, I bring professional ears and outboard hardware processing to every project.

I work across all genres and production styles, from intimate acoustic recordings to full rock and pop productions. Every mix is accompanied by revision rounds until the result is right.

All services are fully remote: You upload your files, I do the work, and I deliver high-resolution audio files ready for release or further production. Contact me for pricing.

How It Works

A simple, collaborative process designed to keep your project moving.

1

Send Your Tracks

Upload your stems, rough mixes, demos, or reference material to a shared folder. Include any notes on the sound you're going for: Genre references, arrangement ideas, whatever helps paint the picture.

2

We Talk It Through

We connect via Zoom or phone to discuss your vision, the vibe of the track, specific instrument choices, and timeline. This conversation is where we align before any recording or mixing begins.

3

I Get to Work

I record parts and/or mix your project in my studio. For session work, I send you rough checks before committing to final takes. For mixing, I send check mixes along the way so you're never in the dark about where the track is heading.

4

You Get the Final Product

Final deliverables are high-resolution audio files: Recorded stems, a stereo mix, a completed master, or all of the above — ready for release, distribution, or continued production on your end.
