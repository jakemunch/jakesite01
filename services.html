Services

Music lessons, audio engineering instruction, and live sound consulting. All tailored to your level and goals.

🎵

Music Lessons

Private, one-on-one instruction available via Zoom or in-person in the Greater Philadelphia area. All ages welcome. I meet you where you are musically and build a path toward where you want to be.

🥁 Drumset

Genres: Jazz, rock, funk, R&B, metal, pop, Latin, and more

Levels: Beginner through professional

From basic grip and coordination to advanced independence, odd meters, and genre-specific vocabulary. I'll build your musicianship from the ground up or help you break through a plateau.

🎼 Classical Percussion

Instruments: Snare drum (marching & concert), xylophone, marimba, vibraphone, timpani, and auxiliary percussion

Levels: Beginner through collegiate

Focused technique, sight-reading, rudimental mastery, and audition preparation for school ensembles, drum corps, and college-level programs.

🎸 Acoustic Guitar

Styles: Folk, blues, fingerstyle, slide, rock, singer-songwriter

Levels: Beginner through collegiate

Chord shapes, strumming, fingerpicking, open tunings, slide technique, and repertoire building. Learn songs you love while developing strong foundational skills.

⚡ Electric Guitar

Styles: Blues, rock, metal, R&B, funk

Levels: Beginner through collegiate

Technique, tone, gear selection, effects, improvisation, and genre vocabulary. From your first power chord to advanced soloing concepts.

🎹 Piano / Keyboard

Genres: All genres

Levels: Beginner through intermediate

Reading music, chord voicings, accompaniment patterns, and music theory as applied to the keyboard. Foundational skills which can be applied to any other instrument.

Lesson format: Available via Zoom or in-person · All ages · Contact me for pricing · View available instruments & studio gear

🎛️

Audio Engineering & Design Instruction

Learn the art and science of professional audio, whether your goal is studio recording, live sound, or both.

🎚️ Studio Audio Engineering

Topics: Signal flow, microphone technique, recording, mixing, DAW workflows, and production

Levels: Beginner through professional

From understanding gain structure to delivering a polished mix. I teach the concepts and the critical listening skills that make the difference between amateur and professional results.

🔊 Live Pro Audio Engineering

Topics: Console operation, system design, mixing live sound, wireless microphone technology, RF coordination, and troubleshooting

Levels: Beginner through professional

Hands-on knowledge for anyone working in live events, theater, houses of worship, or touring. Special emphasis on wireless technology and real-world problem solving.

Lesson format: Available via Zoom or in-person · Contact me for pricing

🎭

Live Sound Consulting & Theater Sound Design

Professional consulting services for organizations that need expert audio oversight, system design, or ongoing sound support.

Sound System Assessment & Design

Evaluation of your current audio setup with actionable recommendations for improvement; whether you're upgrading a school auditorium, outfitting a community theater, or planning a new installation.

Theatrical Production Sound Design

Full sound design services for musicals and plays, including wireless mic plots, speaker placement, effects design, and mix strategy. I work with your team from pre-production through closing night.

Wireless & RF Coordination

Specialized expertise in multi-channel wireless microphone deployments. Frequency coordination, interference troubleshooting, and system optimization using professional tools and techniques.

Mentoring & Training

On-site training for student crews, volunteer operators, and staff. I don't just set up and leave...I build your team's ability to run the system confidently and independently.

Serving: Schools, community theaters, houses of worship, and event venues in the Greater Philadelphia area · Contact me to discuss your project · View available wireless equipment
